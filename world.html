Galldur - A Tabletop Role-Playing Game Setting

Galldur is a personal project of mine, born out of my interest in tabletop role-playing games and gamemastering. It is currently a work in progress, but I am always adding more to it.

Details

Details

Vaguely inspired by Numenera, Galldur is a world fraught with magic and mystery. The full details of Galldur are on the Worldanvil website, though it is a work in progress. The project is mostly an outlet for my creativity and gives me a way to practice my writing. I am currently running a Pathfinder game in this world setting, game mastering weekly games (another hobby).
