Cows and Bulls Team Project

A group project assigned in my Computer Architecture course, our task was to make a fully functional Cows and Bulls game in MIPS Assembly. Working with three other people we created (as far as we're aware) a bug-free and working Cows and Bulls computer game, complete with a full dictionary of 4 letter words to guess from.

Details

The project lasted for around two months and our group met more or less weekly to give status updates, talk about potential problems and plan things out. The role I played in the team was substantial as I was the team leader. I handled duties such as integrating teammates code, delegating work, general structure and design and managing the GitHub repository. In terms of actual coding challenge, this project was a breeze (besides translating everything into MIPS) but the experience I gained as a leader and working in a team was far more valuable.
